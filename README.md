## 🔍 Understanding the Data

The World Happiness Report uses several criteria to rank countries. These criteria include:

* GDP per capita: The economic strength of the nation.
* Social support: The strength of local community networks.
* Healthy life expectancy: The average health of citizens.
* Freedom: The ability of people to make life choices.
* Generosity: The level of charity within the nation.
* Perceptions of corruption: How residents view their government.

The application combines these metrics to generate a final score. You can filter this information by year or by specific country name within the interface.
